Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site will be to help connect business in the local area connect with consumers and other business professionals. It will help make those consumers aware of the goods and services that a certin businesses has to offer. This site will showcase all the businesses that have partnered with the Chamber of Commerce and allow those businesses to include advertisements on the website, as well as notify the public of community events that a particular business may be hosting.

Audience

The target audience for this website will be business owners and other business professionals seeking to network with each other in the local area. It will include those seeking to exchange goods, services, and ideas in order to help their own businesses or careers grow. The audience will also include those who are seeking employment or a career change.
