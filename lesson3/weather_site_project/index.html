Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide individuals and families with up-to-date information on current weather patterns and a weekly forecast to plan your week. This site will also provide its users with news on upcoming outdoor community activities, with suggestions on how to prepare for those activities in a variety of weather conditions. Lastly, this site will serve a purpose for those who wish to tap into their inner-photographer spirit, by offering a chance to win "Photo of the Week" through submission of a photograph via email. The content of this photograph can range from a scenic landscape, a community event, or maybe even a picture of the stars! Only requirement is that the photograph must be taken within the city limits.
